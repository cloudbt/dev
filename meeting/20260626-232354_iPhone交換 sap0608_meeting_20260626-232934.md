*   The identified list of ~1400 broadcast system records with potential resource ID duplication will be further investigated for current status and cannot be used for new applications.

*   A coordinated group day for iPhone replacement will be scheduled in September, tentatively on September 11th or 18th, around lunchtime.

*   The full extent of resource ID duplication and its impact on applications is still being assessed.
*   SRE request content (specific OS/Kernel versions, estimated downtime) needs to be finalized, and combining OS and Kernel updates might require a special SRE template, potentially leading to complications or rejections.
*   Application teams may not be fully aware of the impact of OS/Kernel updates prior to their verification phase.
*   Limited SRE slots necessitate early booking, even if details are not fully confirmed.
*   iPhone replacement requires a full-time employee to facilitate access to the hub area.

**Open Questions:**
*   Are the ~1400 identified broadcast system records currently duplicated, or have they been cleaned up?

*   Is it feasible and advisable to combine OS and Kernel updates into a single SRE request, and what template would be used?
*   What are the specific target versions for the OS and Kernel updates?
